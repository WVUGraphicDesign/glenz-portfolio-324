Donut Stress Event

For this project, I was a part of the Graphic Design team of my group. Our group had to create an event for the WVU Art Museum and decided on the name “Donut Stress” as a way to help students wind down before finals and enjoy viewing art and decorating donuts.

The artwork on display was heavily influenced by the 1960’s and 70’s art movements, and because our groups theme revolved around food, I wanted our logo to have a diner feeling and be fun and soft. Pink was a color that I definitely wanted to include, and my other group members helped decide on the full color palette as well as the font choices. I even went as far as to design a few icons for direction sheets and a PowerPoint that was used during the event.
